About

I’m Zack Gemmell, a Lead Product Designer at Raken working on growth. I’ve been at Raken since early 2019.

Before joining Raken, I was co-founder at Code Cartel and was responsible for designing a wide range of client projects.

I'm most passionate about mobile product, but have extensive experience across web and desktop too. I'm interested in developing design systems and working closely with developers to produce quality digital products.

When I'm not working, I enjoy coding and mentoring aspiring designers.

The best way to reach me is via LinkedIn.
