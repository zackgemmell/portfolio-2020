About

Currently at Loancrate. Previously founding designer at Lindy.

I'm a product designer with a builder's bias. Over the last decade I've been a founding designer at startups, and along the way I've shipped my own apps — designing and coding them end to end. I care most about the small details that make software feel fast, honest, and obvious to use.

I like working closely with founders and engineers, moving quickly from a rough idea to something real in front of users. When I'm not working, I'm usually surfing or building something small.
